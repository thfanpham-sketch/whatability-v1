Our Story

Why What Ability Foundation exists.

What Ability Foundation was established by aspiring NRL player Steve Dresler to fund and facilitate experiences for people living with a disability and their families across Australia.

We exist to ease the financial pressure on people with a disability and their families when it comes to accessing their community. Often parents are unable to work, and pressure on household budgets means many families can't afford to access community events or activities that others take for granted.

That's why we partner with experience providers right across the country, unlocking free tickets to the zoo, the local arcade, theme parks and major sporting events, so a disability never decides who gets to feel included.

Our Promise

100% of all personal donations go to the cause.

How will we deliver on this? Since launching, we've maintained two separate bank accounts: one for operational costs and one exclusively for funding experiences. Day-to-day operations are funded through corporate sponsorships and grants, so every personal donation goes directly to tickets and experiences.

How It Works

We unlock free experiences. Here's how.

1

Register once

Submit a quick application to join our community. We'll ask for a few details to confirm eligibility.

2

Get approved

Our team will review your application and send you a welcome email once you're approved.

3

Receive experiences

Once approved, you'll receive a monthly email with all the experiences on for the month ahead.

4

Pick your experience

Each month, we'll email available experiences in your area. Simply click to request one.

5

We handle the rest

No stress, no cost. Our team books the tickets and sends them to you. 100% free.
