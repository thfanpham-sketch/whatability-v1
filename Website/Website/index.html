Why we exist

A simple belief: every family deserves happy experiences.

Founded by aspiring NRL player Steve Dresler, What Ability Foundation funds and facilitates experiences for people living with a disability and their families across Australia.

V

Vision

To create an inclusive world.

P

Purpose

To bring happiness to people living with a disability and their families.

M

Mission

To unlock 1,000,000 experiences for people with disabilities and their families.

I

Impact

25,000+ experiences unlocked to date, and counting.

What we do

We partner with experience providers across Australia.

We unlock tickets for people with disabilities and their families: a trip to the zoo, the local arcade, a theme park or a sporting event. Individuals apply, and our team handles the rest.

  • ✓Free to apply. No cost to families, ever.
  • ✓Monthly emails with new experiences in your area.
  • ✓100% of personal donations go directly to experiences.

Our promise

100% of all personal donations go to the cause.

We keep two separate bank accounts: one for operational costs (funded by corporate sponsorships and grants) and one exclusively for funding experiences. Every personal donation goes directly to tickets and outings.
